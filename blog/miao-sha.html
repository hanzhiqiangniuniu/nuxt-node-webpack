10 Incredibly Smart Tips For Surviving Long Flights

Josh  / Jun 2, 2017 01:59:43 PM

秒杀 miǎo shā 

-----------------------

[Surface Meaning]

--------------------

秒(miǎo): Second

--------------------

杀(shā): Kill. Sometimes Chinese use 

"Kill Price" to describe price bargain.

--------------------

秒杀(miǎo shā)：Kill in seconds. 

-----------------------

[Explanation]

---------------------- 

1. In a game, 秒杀(miǎo shā) is used 

when players are fighting against each 

other or a monster, since the other 

party is extraordinarily strong and 

given that players are killed instantly, 

in a split second, before they are able 

to fight back or escape.

-----------------------

2.秒杀(miǎo shā) in on-line group buying

means that buyers snap one particular 

article up immediately. Fast buyers may 

not get the cheaper article. 

-----------------------

-----------------------

[Example] 

-----------------------

哈哈，你玩游戏的时候怎么老被秒杀(miǎo shā)呀，下次动作快点！

Aha, you are often 秒杀(miǎo shā) when 

playing a game. Act faster next time!

-----------------------



-----------------------

-----------------------

我们上网秒杀(miǎo shā)去! 

Let's go 秒杀(miǎo shā) online!

-----------------------
